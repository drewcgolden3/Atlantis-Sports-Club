Presale questions

What does “locked for life” mean?

As long as your founding membership stays active, your monthly rate never increases — even as regular rates go up over the years.

What happens after 500 members?

The founding offer closes. New members join at the regular rate of $299 down and $129/month.

When do I start paying?

You reserve your spot now with $99 down. Monthly billing begins when the club opens on July 7, 2027.

Can I cancel?

Yes — you can cancel any time before opening day. There’s no enrollment fee.
